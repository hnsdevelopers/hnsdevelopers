Flyer Designing Documentation

What is Flyer Designing?
Flyer designing involves creating informative and visually appealing marketing materials that can be distributed physically or digitally. Flyers are used to advertise events, products, or services.
Key Elements of Flyer Design
Flyers typically include:
1. Attention-grabbing headline
2. Engaging visuals
3. Key information about the offer or event
4. Contact information and CTA
Best Practices
To design effective flyers:
- Use bold colors to capture attention
- Keep the message clear and easy to read
- Include relevant contact details and a CTA

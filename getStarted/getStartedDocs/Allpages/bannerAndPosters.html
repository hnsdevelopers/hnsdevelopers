Banners and Posters Documentation

What are Banners and Posters?
Banners and posters are visual tools used in marketing, advertisements, and events to communicate a message or promote a brand, product, or service.
Differences Between Banners and Posters
While both are used for promotion, there are distinct differences:
1. Banners are typically large, horizontal, and used for outdoor marketing.
2. Posters are usually smaller, vertical, and used indoors or for specific announcements.
Common Uses of Banners and Posters
Banners and posters can be used for various purposes:
- Event promotions
- Product launches
- Brand awareness campaigns
- Political campaigns
Design Tips for Banners and Posters
Here are some tips for creating effective banners and posters:
- Use bold, eye-catching colors
- Keep the message clear and concise
- Include a strong call-to-action
- Utilize high-quality images and graphics

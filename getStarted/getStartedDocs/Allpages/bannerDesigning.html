Banner Designing Documentation

What is Banner Designing?
Banner designing involves creating visually compelling banners for promotional purposes. These banners can be physical, like outdoor advertisements, or digital, used on websites or social media.
Key Elements of Banner Design
Banner designs usually include:
1. Clear and bold visuals
2. Engaging headline or tagline
3. Brand elements like logos and colors
4. Strong call-to-action (CTA)
Best Practices
To design effective banners:
- Use high-resolution images
- Keep the message clear and concise
- Make sure the banner stands out visually

Flex and Hoardings Documentation

What are Flex and Hoardings?
Flex and hoardings are large-scale outdoor advertising materials used to capture public attention. Flex refers to flexible, vinyl-based printed material, while hoardings are large billboards placed in high-traffic areas.
Uses of Flex and Hoardings
These advertising tools are commonly used for the following purposes:
1. Brand promotions
2. Political campaigns
3. Event advertising
4. Product launches
Types of Flex and Hoardings
Flex and hoardings come in different formats depending on the advertising requirements:
- Front-lit and back-lit flex
- Digital hoardings (LED boards)
- Static hoardings
- Mobile hoardings (mounted on vehicles)
Design Tips for Flex and Hoardings
To create effective flex and hoardings, consider these design tips:
- Use bold and bright colors for visibility
- Keep the message short and clear
- Include the brand logo prominently
- Ensure readability from a distance

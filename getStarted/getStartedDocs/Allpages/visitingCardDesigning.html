Visiting Card Designing Documentation

What is Visiting Card Designing?
Visiting card designing involves creating small cards that carry essential information about a person or a company. These cards are used for networking and providing contact details in a professional manner.
Key Elements of Visiting Card Design
Effective visiting cards typically include:
1. Name and designation
2. Company logo
3. Contact information (phone, email, address)
4. Website and social media handles
Best Practices
To design impactful visiting cards:
- Use a clean and professional layout
- Choose readable fonts and appropriate colors
- Ensure that all information is up to date
- Consider including a QR code for easy access to digital profiles
